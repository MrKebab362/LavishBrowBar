About Our Beauty Salon

We are a sub-branch within the larger Lavish Beauty Salon at Whestone, London: Lavish Brow Bar. We offer a range of services from tinting, shaping, hot wax and threading; all the way to scrubs, waxing and cleansing. We are a small team of experts dedicated to making sure you get exactly the results you came in for. We are a no-nonsense brow bar, so we're committed to make sure your experience is as enjoyable and smooth as possible
